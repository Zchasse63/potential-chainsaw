Privacy Notice

Last updated: July 17, 2026 · Interim version

This is an interim privacy notice. A complete notice, reviewed by counsel, will replace it before member-facing services launch.

Who we are

Kelo is studio-operations software used by wellness studios to manage bookings, memberships, payments, and member communications. It is operated from Tampa, Florida, USA.

What we collect

  • Contact details you or your studio provide: name, email address, phone number.
  • Booking, attendance, membership, and purchase records for the studio you attend.
  • Messages the studio sends you (email and SMS) and their delivery status.

Text messaging (SMS)

  • SMS is sent only for studio business: booking confirmations, account notices, and — only with your consent — marketing messages.
  • Message frequency varies; message and data rates may apply.
  • Reply STOP to opt out at any time, or HELP for help. Opt-outs are honored immediately and permanently unless you re-subscribe.
  • No mobile information is shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes.

How we use information

To operate the studio's services: manage bookings and memberships, process payments (via Stripe — Kelo never stores card numbers), send service communications, and produce business reports for the studio. We do not sell personal information.

Contact

Questions or requests about your data: contact the studio you attend, or email zchasse89@gmail.com.
