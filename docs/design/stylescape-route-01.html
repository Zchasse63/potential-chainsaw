The quiet instrument.

A professional tool in birch-smoke grays and slate blue — cool, machined, legible. Warmth doesn't come from color; it comes from material moments, generous space, and type with a human hand. Where data lives, Kelo reads like a calibrated gauge: hairline rules, tabular numerals, provenance on every figure. Truth-telling, made visible.

ASSUMPTIONS & READINGS OF THE BRIEF
  • Warmth dial set at ~35/100 — instrument first, sauna in the undertone.
  • No cedar reds/oranges; brand accent is smoke slate. Birch tan is a material, used sparingly, never a status color.
  • Two Scandinavian text faces + one mono; all SIL OFL, commercial-safe.
  • Imported (Glofox-era) data looks physically different from Kelo-native data — texture, not color alone.
  • Sauna Guys assets pending → white-label slots shown as placeholders.

Money states differ by shape + icon + fill, never color alone: confirmed is a soft pill, processing is squared and outlined (still moving), failed is sharp and solid (loudest thing on any screen), refund-pending is dashed (money in transit back).

RULES
  • 4px baseline grid; line-heights are multiples of 4.
  • Numbers in running UI text stay Schibsted; numbers that mean money, time, or identity go mono. The switch itself signals “this is a reading off the gauge.”
  • Familjen never below 20px; it's a voice, not a workhorse.
  • Two families max in app chrome — Familjen appears once per screen.
